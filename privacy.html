Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 March 2026  •  Effective: 28 March 2026

TaskNext.AI (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it when you use our service at tasknextai.one.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information — name, email address, and password when you register
  • Billing information — payment details processed securely via Stripe (we never store card numbers)
  • Connected accounts — access tokens for social platforms you choose to connect (Instagram, X, LinkedIn, TikTok)
  • Content you create — captions, images, and schedules generated or uploaded within the platform
  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, and actions taken within the app

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the TaskNext.AI service
  • Process payments and manage your subscription
  • Post content to your connected social accounts on your instruction
  • Send you important service updates and account notifications
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Analyse usage patterns to improve the product (anonymised and aggregated)

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We never use your content to train AI models. We share data only with:

  • Stripe — for payment processing (card details never stored by us)
  • Supabase (PostgreSQL) — our database and authentication provider; all data encrypted at rest
  • Anthropic — AI content generation (prompts only, no personal identifiers sent)
  • Zernio — social media post scheduling and publishing on your behalf
  • Google — OAuth authentication (we receive read-only tokens; we never store your Google password)
  • Social platforms — only the content you explicitly instruct us to post
  • Legal obligations — where required by law or to protect our legal rights

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your subscription, we retain your data for 30 days to allow for account recovery, then delete it permanently. You may request immediate deletion by contacting us.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies required for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. We use anonymous analytics (aggregated only) to understand how the product is used.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, hashed passwords, and encrypted storage for OAuth tokens. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children

TaskNext.AI is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16. If you believe we have collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or via a notice within the app. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

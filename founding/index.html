Things worth asking.

What happens after the 100 seats fill? +
The founding rate disappears. Public pricing starts at £29/mo for Growth and will breathe upward as Ory's capabilities deepen. Founding members never see a price change. Ever. That's the promise, coded into the billing layer, not just the marketing page.
Why is this an invite-only cohort? +
Because founding members shape what Ory becomes. I read your first three posts personally, Ory learns from how I respond, and that process works best at small scale.
Can I downgrade or cancel? +
Yes. Cancel anytime, from your billing page, in two clicks. 30-day refund, no questions. If you cancel and return within 90 days, your founding rate is still yours.
Can I switch tiers later? +
Yes, at any time. If you move up, you pay the founding rate of the new tier — £14.99 Growth, £29 Pro, £79 Unlimited — not the public price. If you move down, same rule. Your founding discount follows you across tiers for life.
What does "Brand Brain" actually do? +
It reads the three posts of yours that performed best, studies the rhythm of your sentences, the words you avoid, the punctuation you use as breath. Then Ory writes drafts that sound like you on a good day — never generic, never "excited to share".
Is my data used to train anything? +
No. Your voice memory lives in your Brand Brain, scoped to your account. We don't train foundation models on member data. We don't share it with other members. Ory learns you, privately.

01 · the four sacred verbs

Ory does only four things. Nothing more.

Every feature in this product is rooted in one of these words. If a request doesn't fit here, it doesn't ship. That's the discipline.

01
breathes

Posts at the rhythm your audience actually reads — not the rhythm algorithms demand. Cadence matched to attention, not anxiety.

02
learns

Studies your three best-performing posts, your voice, your silences. Refines in the background. Never asks you to fill another form.

03
heals

Catches typos, tonal drift, the risky late-night draft. Gentle guardrails for your reputation, before it leaves your keyboard.

04
grows

Reach expands at the pace of trust. Deep engagement with 200 readers over shallow metrics with 20,000 strangers.

02 · the honest part

You don't need another app that screams at you to post three times today.

You need something that remembers you're a human who also has clients, deadlines, and a garden to tend.

03 · not another scheduler

A different species of software.

The Buffer / Hootsuite era
Automation theatre
  • Queue-and-forget scheduling disconnected from your voice
  • Generic AI captions that sound like every other SaaS founder
  • Pricing that punishes you for growing
  • Dashboards designed to maximise your time in the dashboard
  • Urgency as a feature, not a bug
The Ory way
A quiet organism
  • A single living system that speaks in your cadence from day three
  • Captions generated, then composted — never shipped generic
  • One founding rate. No per-seat, per-platform, per-post penalty
  • A dashboard you check once on Friday and forget
  • Calm as a feature, not a slogan

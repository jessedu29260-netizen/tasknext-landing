❓ FAQ

Got questions?
We've got answers.

What AI model does TaskNext.AI use?+
TaskNext.AI runs on Claude Sonnet by Anthropic — one of the most capable AI models available. It runs locally on your machine, so your data never leaves your computer.
Do I need any tech skills to use it?+
None at all. TaskNext.AI is designed for creators and marketers, not developers. Everything works through a clean visual interface — no code, no command lines.
Is my data private and secure?+
100%. Because TaskNext.AI runs locally on your machine, your content, credentials, and data never touch any server we control. It's your data, on your hardware.
What social platforms are supported?+
Instagram, X (Twitter), LinkedIn, and TikTok — all four supported from day one. More platforms coming in V2.
Are founding member prices really locked forever?+
Yes. Whatever price you sign up at today is your price for as long as you stay subscribed. We will never raise your rate. This is our promise to early supporters.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Absolutely. No contracts, no exit fees. Cancel in one click from your account page. We'd rather earn your loyalty than trap you.
